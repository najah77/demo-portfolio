Biography

Naseef Gafoor is a photographer based out of Bangalore and Kochi. He specialises mainly in hotel / resort, people and product photography

Post graduated in Professional Photography from Light & Life Academy, his eye for detailing and describing with the use of lights and minimalistic settings are evident through his works, be it professional or personal. He is quite keen on exploring and learning through the challenges in the field.

Selected clients : TAJ Hotels & Resorts, Asian Paints, Murugappa Group, Olam food ingredients, Synthite Industries, JK Tyres, Scotts Garments, Malabar Gold & Diamonds, Bhima Jewellery, Kalyan Silks, Seematti, Classic Coffee, Sobha Builders, Skyline Builders, Asset Homes among many others.
